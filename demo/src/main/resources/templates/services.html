Dining

Hotel Ganesha houses a multi-cuisine restaurant where you can sample mouthwatering Indian, Chinese and Continental cuisines.

Our dining hall adds an exotic and sttvic flavor to your visit. Pamper your taste buds at the ethnic delights like Gutta curry & Ker Sangri, for the less adventurous; continental and Chinese cuisine is readily available. Dining hall has seating capacity of 80 people and serves hygienic cuisine. We organise bar-be-Que dinners where our guests enjoy the live cultural shows and the warmth of bonfires.

Breakfast is served between 8:30 am and 10:30 am. Tea and coffee with cookies served before and after the safari at dining hall.

Lunch is served between 1:00 pm and 2:30 pm.

Dinner is served between 7:30 pm and 9:30 pm. Barbecues can be arranged on request at the lawn.
